Korea - Last week

My last week at Korea. Quite a photo dump and a read.

DDP light exhibition. Didn't know DDP had a nice reading space with magazines.

Yonsei University. Great campus, clubs, and people.

Gyeongju. Amazing national museum and many nice places to walk and see.

Once again, amazing national museum.

Thank you so much Korea. It's so cliche but it has genuienly been an amazing semester exchange. I wish I could've stayed longer because now I know so much more things I could've done. Things like Hangang cycling, Bugaksan hiking, actually have conversational level Korean (YOU MUST GET TO THAT LEVEL BEFORE YOU GO, "I'll learn it there" never works), Gwangju, Jeonju, Jeju trip is still in my bucket list, but even with all that not being done yet, my time at Korea was still very fulfilling.

I say this with confidence, "I will be back to Korea". I'm so grateful of the opportunity I've had, and the opportunities I will have in the future. Ultimately, thank you to all my friends and people I've made and met, you've made this something to remember, and I don't doubt we will still make more moments to remember.
