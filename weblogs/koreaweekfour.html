Korea - week 4

This is about week 4, quite an active week

Interesting fire drill by my dormitory. Allowed people to try out the extinguisher, with even fire trucks arriving

Sinchon damjang. 20000KRW, the sign up process was a pain but its a decent size. Was able to onsight a V4 so that's nice.

Mt. Gwanak is the toughest hike (basically climbing at some points) I've ever done. I thought we were dead when some serious snow started, but we were able to rest and had some shin ramyon. Equal parts amazing and scary.
