Korea - week 5

This is about week 5 in Korea

Another Ansan hike. Mostly went around it, so it was a nice calming hike. Did a tough section to take a shortcut to Bongwonsa temple.

The infastrucutre investment Seoul city gives to its hikes always suprises me, it's true that Seoul is a hikers paradise.

Bongwonsa temple is interesting, as shown through the video, bhuddist chants is its thing.

After the hike, decided to try out jimjilbang nearby. The sauna is very different, and it makes you sweat for sure. Personally I prefer steam sauna, so I didn't like the very dry furance and charcoal sauna too much, but the fireplace style and many facilities is a unique and social experience. I'd say it's a fun place to go there as a group.

Food prep. Cooked the brown rice too much, but it's really filling for sure.
