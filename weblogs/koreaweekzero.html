Korea - week 0

This is about my first week at Korea, uni hasn't started yet so its 'week 0'

First day - Early morning flight is not nice. Though Korean Air gave two meals in this 2 hour flight so that was a nice surprise. Got on the bus just in time, but got off at the wrong place so had to heave a luggage through a very tough route.

The dorm is alright. very small space and not that clean, but the heater and water pressure is very good.
