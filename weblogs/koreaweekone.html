Korea - week 1

This is about week 1, my first week of Yonsei University

Crazy week, so many things to do and quite stressul, but there's just as many fun events and meeting new people.

The snow and insane classes felt like a baptism into Korea. For the past week I logically knew that I am in Korea, but I didn't fully proccess or felt that. However, after the class and snow, my mind and body physically felt 'oh, I am in Korea now'.

The proofs/logics courses here are insane, thankkfully the probability and statistics here are not so bad. Not sure but I think highschool students here do proofs and logics instead of stats and probability.
